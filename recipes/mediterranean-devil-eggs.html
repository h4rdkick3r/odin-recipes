mediterranean-devil-eggs

Description

These Mediterranean deviled eggs are made with Greek yogurt, feta, olives, and fresh dill, bringing bold flavors inspired by the coastal regions surrounding the world's largest inland sea.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill
  • 2 tablespoons feta cheese
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped olives
  • 1 teaspoon brine from olive jar
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 pinch smoked paprika, or to taste (optional)

Steps

  1. Slice eggs in half lengthwise and remove yolks; set whites aside.
  2. Mash yolks with a fork in a small bowl. Stir in Greek yogurt, vinegar, dill, feta, olives, olive brine, and garlic powder; mix well.
  3. Spoon or pipe egg yolk mixture into egg whites. Sprinkle with smoked paprika. Refrigerate eggs until serving.
