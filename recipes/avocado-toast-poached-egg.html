avocado-toast-poached-egg

Description

This avocado toast topped with a perfectly poached egg is a simple, wholesome breakfast or brunch option that’s full of healthy fats, protein, and flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole-grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh chives (optional)
  • Red pepper flakes for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Toast the slices of bread until golden and crisp.
  2. Mash the avocado in a small bowl with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Bring a pot of water to a gentle simmer, then poach the eggs for about 3–4 minutes until whites are set but yolks are still runny.
  4. Spread mashed avocado evenly over toasted bread. Top each slice with a poached egg.
  5. Garnish with chives and red pepper flakes if desired. Serve immediately.
